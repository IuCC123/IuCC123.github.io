Reko Radio - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 29, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Reko Radio ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use the Reko Radio Discord bot (the "Service"). Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the Service:

* Discord User ID: This information is provided by Discord and allows us to identify you uniquely within the Discord platform.

* Radio Station Information: The URL of the radio station you choose to listen to is stored temporarily to stream a direct radio in a voice channel. This only counts for the /directplay command. This information is not linked to your Discord User ID and is not persistently stored.

Use of Information

We use the information we collect to:

* Provide and operate the Service, including allowing you to listen to radio stations.

* Improve the user experience and identify popular features.

* Investigate and prevent unauthorized access or use of the Service.

Disclosure of Information

We do not share your personal information with any third parties.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Service. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at rekoradio@gmail.com.
